Basic health bar for InGameObject.

Parameters

maxHealth

Maximal health for the owner of health bar.

backgroundDrawable

Background drawable: z-index is a global health bar z-index (TODO); size is used for health bar size; offset is used for health bar bottom center positioning relative to owner top center.

foregroundDrawable

Foreground drawable: z-index is not used; size is used for height and maximum width used for foreground, zero components will be changed to health bar size; offset is used for foreground positioning relative to health bar position.

animateDuration

Duration in seconds of displayed value movement animation
